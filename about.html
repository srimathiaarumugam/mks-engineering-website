About MKS Engineering & Construction

Building trust through quality engineering and construction solutions

Who We Are

MKS Engineering & Construction is a Puducherry-based engineering and construction company delivering end-to-end solutions across civil, electrical, fabrication, structural, and MEP works. We focus on safe, reliable, and cost-effective project execution.

What We Do

We provide complete engineering services under one roof — from planning and execution to finishing and maintenance. Our team handles residential, commercial, and industrial projects with strict adherence to timelines and quality benchmarks.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable and efficient engineering and construction services that exceed customer expectations through professionalism, skilled workmanship, and continuous improvement.

Our Vision

To become a trusted engineering and construction partner known for quality, safety, and long-term client relationships.
